NHS Workforce Statistics for North East and North Cumbria

Data for July 2025

Author

Maria Garcia

Published

December 3, 2025

Summary

This report shows monthly numbers of NHS Hospital and Community Health Services (HCHS) staff working in NHS Trusts and other core organisations in North East and North Cumbria.

Key facts

All NHS Hospital & Community Health Service (HCHS) staff

83,521 FTE in July 2025

Information

Full-Time Equivalent (FTE) is a standardised measure of the workload of an employed person and allows for the total workforce workload to be expressed in an equivalent number of full-time staff. 1.0 FTE equates to full-time work of 37.5 hours per week.
